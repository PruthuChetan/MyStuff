Cyberbullying is bullying that takes place over digital devices like cell phones, computers, and tablets. Cyberbullying can occur through SMS, Text, and apps, or online in social media, forums, or gaming where people can view, participate in, or share content


What are the effects of cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying Effects. Like all forms of bullying, cyberbullying causes psychological, emotional and physical stress. Each person's response to being bullied is unique, but research has shown some general tendencies. StopBullying.gov reports that youth who are bullied have a higher risk of depression and anxiety.

What are some ways to protect ourselves from cyberbullying?


Unfortunately, as long as there are mean-spirited people, it seems unlikely we’ll ever be able to put a complete stop to cyberbullying.
